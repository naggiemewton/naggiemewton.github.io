about the artist

“AI art helps me use my vision to create a better outcome than what I could create without it.”

Koby is an English student at the University of North Carolina at Greensboro. He has always enjoyed creative endeavors in a variety of fields, including literary and visual art. He attended the Governor’s School of North Carolina for Poetry in 2019, where he first began to hone his signature artistic style: tactile expressions and perversely-satiating worldscapes.

Koby is also affected by protanopia, a type of colorblindness that affects one’s perception of red and green wavelengths. He finds that rather than hindering his ability, his protanopia is an attribute, and that it strengthens his artistic skill.

the programs

Koby uses two AI programs to create his art: NeuralBlender and Art Breeder. NeuralBlender works by taking input in the form of text and searching through a wide database of public images to find images tagged with the given text, then blends them using different formulas. The artistic element of this program lies more in the creative choice of word combination, blending Koby’s experience with literary and visual art. Art Breeder similarly blends images, but gives the user more control over the blend itself, allowing them to activate and deactivate the “genetics” of the blend.
